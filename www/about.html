HOW TO USE THIS APP

Metropolis of Science is all about the history of science and innovation in New York City. You can view all the stories in the main page or sort them by neighborhood. You can also use the map to locate the nearest ones and take a personal walking tour of significant places. This application is produced by students of Columbia University's Science Journalism MA program.
